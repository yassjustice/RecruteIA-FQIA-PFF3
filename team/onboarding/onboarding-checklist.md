# 👋 Team Onboarding Checklist

Welcome to the RecruteIA team! Complete these steps to get started.

## Required Steps

- [ ] Get Notion access from Yassir
- [ ] Navigate to your personal page: **Team → [Your Name]**
- [ ] Watch the onboarding video or read \
otion-workflow-guide.md\
- [ ] Understand the task workflow (To Do → In Progress → Review → Done/Blocked)
- [ ] Review your assigned tasks in the Sprint Backlog
- [ ] Join team communication channel (if applicable)

## Key Rules

1. **Use your personal page** - Don't use the general board view
2. **Never mark tasks as Done** - Only the manager can approve tasks
3. **Read Blocked comments** - Follow the feedback to fix issues
4. **Move to In Progress immediately** - When you start working on a task
